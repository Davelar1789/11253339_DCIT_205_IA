Undergraduate Programmes

Our department provides a diverse range of undergraduate degree programs, including the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with both Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) options. Additionally, we offer the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. To be eligible for admission into our esteemed Computer Science programs at the 200 level, prospective candidates must have successfully completed the following courses during their 100-level studies:

    MATH 121: Algebra and Trigonometry
    MATH 122: Calculus I
    CSCD 101: Introduction to Computer Science I
    CSCD 102: Introduction to Computer Science II
As a unique requirement, each student in the Department of Computer Science is expected to own a personal laptop meeting the following minimum specifications: i3 64-bit Processor 8GB RAM 500GB HD or 256 SSD Embark on your academic journey with us, equipped with the essential foundation and tools to thrive in the dynamic field of computer science.

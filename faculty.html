Entry requirements
• Bachelor of Science in Mathematical Sciences
Mathematical Sciences (Actuarial Science, Biomathematics, Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Physics)
Core: Credit passes in English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science & Social Studies
Electives: Credit passes in Elective Mathematics and any other two subjects
• Bachelor of Science in Information Technology
Core: Credit passes in English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science & Social Studies
Electives: Credit passes in any three subjects
• MSc Computer science
• MPhil Computer science
• Ph.D.



Our esteemed faculty comprises seasoned experts in diverse fields of computer science, bringing a wealth of knowledge and experience to our academic community. With a passion for innovation, our faculty members are dedicated to nurturing the next generation of tech leaders through cutting-edge research and dynamic teaching methods. Explore a vibrant intellectual environment led by our accomplished faculty, who are actively engaged in pioneering research that advances the frontiers of computer science.

Our faculty fosters a collaborative learning atmosphere, encouraging students to explore their potential and excel in a rapidly evolving technological landscape. Meet our distinguished professors, whose expertise spans artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, and more, offering students a comprehensive and forward-looking education. Experience personalized mentorship from our faculty members, who are committed to guiding students in their academic pursuits and career aspirations. Discover a faculty team deeply committed to fostering an inclusive and diverse learning environment, where creativity and innovation thrive. Benefit from a curriculum shaped by the latest industry trends, as our faculty maintains strong connections with leading tech companies, ensuring relevance and real-world applicability. Engage with passionate educators who inspire critical thinking, problem-solving, and a lifelong love for learning within the dynamic realm of computer science. Our faculty's research contributions are driving advancements in areas such as machine learning, software engineering, and human-computer interaction, influencing both academia and industry.

Join a community of learners led by faculty members who not only impart knowledge but also instill a sense of curiosity and a desire for continuous exploration in the ever-evolving field of computer science. Explore the unique perspectives and diverse backgrounds of our faculty, creating a rich tapestry of expertise that enriches the educational experience for every student.
